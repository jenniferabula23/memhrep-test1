Achievements
"Don't be ashamed to share your story it will inspire others to speak up"



Mental Health Education

From 2018-2022 ,about 150 school-based mental health clubs were formed in Ashanti and Upper West region. Also about 306 health teacher were given basic training on common mental disorders.

Community Mental Health Support

The organization currently takes care and support over 50 service users whose medical and personal needs are catered for.

Mental Health Outreach

Persons with mental health needs in around the community MEMHREP operates have been given intensive care and have return to work activitues after treatments

Covid-19 Support

During the covid-19 pandemic lockdown, MEMHREP donated essential items to the Minitry of Gender, Children and Social Protection to be distributed to persons with mental health needs on the street of Kumasi .


Agricultural Program

Acres of land was acquired to produce food that was used to feed persons suffering from mental illness. As a result, the organization won the District National Farmers Award in 2014

Volunteering and Home Support

We paid regular home visit to people with mental health needs to find out how they were responding to treatment


Street Outreach

The organization from its little resources was able to feed a minimum of 300-350 persons every month in various part of Ghana. In 2020,MEMHREP also collaborated with feed the sick foundation to feed about 100 persons living on the streets of kasoa Ghana

Memhrep Tv

2022 saw the launch of MEMHREP's youtube channel "Memhrep Tv" geared at educating the public on mental health. Mental health education intervies, Movies, Documentries have since been produced

FEE (Feed,Educate,Employ)

Persons suffering from mild symptons of mental illness were located in Kumasi,Ghana and were given medical attention, fed ,groomed and employed.

Rehabilitation Center

A five acre land has been acquired to build a rehabilitation center for our service users in Ghana(Africa). This facility upon completion will cater for about 500-600 In-Patients and Out-patients.
