Countries Of Operations
Since 2011, MEMHREP together with its donors and partners have been operating in three continents. We work tirelessly to provide medical treatments, counselling and emotional support to persons with mental health needs and other vulnerabilities.





Africa

Over the years, MEMHREP together with it partners have progressively worked to curb mental health illness in developing countries. Currently, we operate on three main modules; Prevention, Care and Support(PCS).
